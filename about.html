About Us

History :

Reynolds Pens has been inspiring writers, artists, and professionals for over 80 years with high-quality, affordable writing instruments. Founded in 1945, Reynolds began with a vision to make durable, stylish pens accessible to everyone. Our pens have since become staples in homes, schools, and offices around the world.

Driven by a commitment to innovation, we continuously develop pens that meet the evolving needs of our users—whether it’s smooth writing for everyday tasks, precision for artistic endeavors, or reliable performance for professionals. At Reynolds, we believe that the power of writing lies in its ability to capture thoughts, ideas, and dreams. Every pen we create is designed to make that process seamless and enjoyable.

We are proud to be a part of your story, providing tools that empower self-expression and creativity. Thank you for choosing Reynolds Pens as your writing partner.

R&D :

Reynolds has identified that blue ink, finer lines and smaller diameter pens are preferred by Indian consumers because they offer a comfortable grips. Additionally, the concept of refillable pens, which is unique to India as compared to some other major countries, is appreciated by all who go to their nearest stationery shop to buy an extra refill for their favourite pens. With this, we ensure that even the most minute needs of our customers are taken care of.

The strictest quality control norms, extensive engineering, and a deep understanding of the Indian consumer ensure we create the best quality products at affordable prices. Constant innovation is carried out by Reynolds even in fields of colour expansion and ink development so we can continue offering exciting products to our customers.

Along with Reynolds, we manufacture for other global Newell Brands and businesses such as Rotring, Parker, Waterman, and others.

"Newell Brands is the parent company of Reynolds."
